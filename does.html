Senior Does

Little Orchard NL Jolene (Pedigree)

Jolene has one leg of her ADGA championship, and produces approximately 8-10 lbs milk per day. She has a very sweet temperament, and produces roughly 1oz of cashmere with a 1" staple length. She's never had a problem kidding, although she prefers to have someone holding her head in their lap for moral support.

Little Orchard NL Lorraine (Pedigree)

Lorraine has an excellent udder, and produces approximately 8-10 lbs milk per day. She's an exceptionally easy kidder with strong maternal instincts; she's dam raised all of her kids with no issues.

Junior Does

Golden Gnome Roxanne (Pedigree)

Sire: The Everlasting Farm Pepe

Dam: Little Orchard NL Lorraine

Roxanne is a young first freshener, but shaping up very nicely. She had an easy time kidding and no trouble dam raising (just like her dam in that respect). She has very nice feet that rarely need a trim, with a sweet and docile temperament. She's on the smaller side (her sire has everything but size), but still giving approximately 6 lbs of milk a day.

Golden Gnome Layla (Pedigree)

Sire: The Everlasting Farm Pepe

Dam: Little Orchard NL Lorraine

Layla is developing nicely; current breeding plans are to pair her with Yoshi this year. She has excellent feet and makes very efficient use of her feed (with nothing but pasture and hay, she's still carrying a few extra pounds!)

Golden Gnome Billie Jean (Pedigree)

Sire: The Everlasting Farm Pepe

Dam: Little Orchard NL Jolene

Billie Jean is developing nicely; current breeding plans are to pair her with Yoshi this year. She's filling out into a nice long doe, which should make future kidding nice and easy. She can be a bit bossy with other goats, and needs a strong hand on the collar when being led, but fortunately doesn't pick fights (with either goats or people). Good feet, and she produced a good amount of cashmere as a kid (over an ounce at 8 months old) with a nice long staple.
