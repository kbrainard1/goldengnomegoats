Senior Does

Little Orchard NL Jolene (Pedigree)

Jolene has one leg of her ADGA championship, and produces approximately 8-10 lbs milk per day. She has a very sweet temperament, and produces roughly 1oz of cashmere with a 1" staple length. No issues with parasites, and has never had a problem kidding

Little Orchard NL Lorraine (Pedigree)

Lorraine has an excellent udder, and produces approximately 8-10 lbs milk per day. No isses with parasites, and she's an exceptionally easy kidder with strong maternal instincts. Has dam raised all of her kids with no issues.

Golden Gnome Roxanne (Pedigree)

Sire: The Everlasting Farm Pepe

Dam: Little Orchard NL Lorraine

Roxanne is a young first freshener, but shaping up very nicely. Easy kidding and no trouble dam raising (just like her dam in that respect). No issues with parasites, very nice feet that rarely need a trim, sweet and docile temperament. She's on the smaller side (her sire has everything but size), but still giving approximately 6 lbs of milk a day.
