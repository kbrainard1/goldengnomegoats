Senior Does

Little Orchard NL Jolene (Pedigree)

Jolene has one leg of her ADGA championship, and comfortably produces at milk star levels (unfortunately we're not on DHIR test, so her official milk star is waiting on finding a nearby one-day milk test). She has a very sweet temperament, and produces roughly 1oz of cashmere with a 1" staple length. She's never had a problem kidding, although she prefers to have someone holding her head in their lap for moral support. She's a very large doe, so her kids would be well-suited for a dual dairy/pack goat situation. One of her 2025 kids is currently in training here to be a harness goat!

Production Records:

2024: 1650 lbs

Kidding Records:

2023: 1 doeling

2024: 2 bucklings

2025: 1 doeling, 1 buckling

Weight (taped):

175 lbs

Show Results:

2023 Ulster County Fair: Best in Breed

2025 Ulster County Fair: Best in Breed

Golden Gnome Roxanne (Pedigree)

Sire: The Everlasting Farm Pepe

Dam: Little Orchard NL Lorraine

Roxanne is a model of a low-maintenance goat. She's an easy kidder and fantastic mom (just like her dam in that respect). She has very nice feet that rarely need a trim, with a sweet and docile temperament. She's on the smaller side (her sire has everything but size), but still giving approximately 6 lbs of milk a day.

Kidding Records:

2024: 1 buckling

2025: 1 doeling

Show Results:

2025 Ulster County Fair: Lamancha Reserve Champion

Golden Gnome Billie Jean (Pedigree)

Sire: The Everlasting Farm Pepe

Dam: Little Orchard NL Jolene

Billie Jean is developing nicely; current breeding plans are to pair her with Yoshi again this year. She's filling out into a nice long doe with a capacious udder. She can be a bit bossy with other goats, and needs a strong hand on the collar when being led, but fortunately doesn't pick fights (with either goats or people). Good feet, and she produced a good amount of cashmere as a kid (over an ounce at 8 months old) with a nice long staple.

Kidding Records:

2025: 1 doeling, 1 buckling
